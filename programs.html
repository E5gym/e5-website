All paths.
One
system.

Every E5 program shares a common foundation: strength, coordination, awareness, and respect for movement. From there, students find their path.

Training Pathways

Competition Track

Agile, resilient athletes training toward local and Colorado parkour and ninja competitions, with advanced athletes supported for national-level events as skill and commitment grow.

Performance Track

Expressive, disciplined performers developing through acrobatics, aerial arts, and movement. Students train toward showcases and performances, with advanced opportunities at regional and national levels.

Camp Friday

Fridays
covered.

Camp Friday is a structured training session designed for kids who are out of school on Fridays. It combines movement-based activities, games, and skill-building in a safe, supervised environment. Parents can choose monthly membership or drop-in sessions.

  • Structured training + games for active engagement
  • High-energy, supervised environment for safety and fun
  • Social + physical development through group activities
  • Flexible options: drop-in or monthly membership

Ages 5–12 Most Popular

NinFit

Parkour · Tumbling · Ninja Training

Our flagship program. NinFit fuses parkour, tumbling, trampoline, and ninja training into a structured 9-level progression system. Kids earn levels, not just stickers.

  • Parkour vaults, jumps, and precision movement
  • Tumbling and acrobatic foundations
  • Trampoline and aerial awareness
  • Character development woven into every class
  • Beginner: Mon–Thurs 4:30–5:30 PM

Ages 5–12

Tx3

Tumbling · Trampoline · Tricking

For kids who cannot get enough flips. Tx3 spends the full hour on ground acrobatics, trampoline mechanics, and tricking — combining skill with style and creativity.

  • Tumbling from foundations to advanced
  • Trampoline mechanics and air awareness
  • Tricking: kicks, flips, and combos
  • 5-level progression system
  • Mon and Tues 5:30–6:30 PM

Ages 8+

Aerial Silks

Grace · Strength · Expression

Students develop upper body strength, spatial awareness, and artistic expression through structured aerial training. Beginner classes are open to all. Intermediate is by invitation only.

  • Climbs, inversions, and hangs
  • Knots, locks, wraps, and drops
  • Choreography and performance readiness
  • Strength conditioning built into every class
  • Beginner: Tues 5:30 PM  ·  Intermediate: Mon 5:30 PM
Ages 3–5

Mini Ninjas

Movement · Play · Foundation

Where it all begins. Mini Ninjas builds fundamental movement, courage, and confidence through play-based obstacle courses and guided acrobatics. Big development in small bodies.

  • Gross motor and body awareness foundations
  • Introductory tumbling and jumping skills
  • Social skills: turns, directions, and peer respect
  • Character seeds planted from day one
  • Friday 9:00–10:00 AM
Ages 0–3 Parent Participation

Ninja Tots

Parent and Child · Open Gym

Bond with your little one in a safe, structured open gym environment. A coach guides families through obstacles, shares spotting techniques, and encourages early curiosity and courage.

  • Mon and Wed 9:00 to 11:00 AM
  • Friday 10:00 to 11:30 AM
  • Big squishy mats and a colorful environment
  • No experience needed. Just show up.

Advanced Tracks

Three tracks. Each aligned to an element. Each representing a different direction an E5 athlete can grow.

Air  ·  Performance Track

E5 Echo

E5's elite performance troupe. Echo is aligned with Air — creativity, expression, and lightness in movement. Athletes train year-round with live performances and travel. Auditions include a panel interview and skill gate.

Audition Required Year-Round

Water  ·  Competition Track

Surge

Surge is aligned with Water — adaptability, flow, and resilience under pressure. The competition development track for athletes preparing for parkour and ninja-style events at local, state, and national levels.
