The Pathway

More Than Skills.
A Pathway Into Performance.

Echo is designed for students who are ready to take their training seriously and explore what it means to perform. Students continue developing strength, flexibility, tumbling, aerial technique, and acrobatic control — but the deeper focus is learning how to turn movement into a polished performance.

This is where Elemental Athletes step into a new level of commitment, creativity, and presence. Echo is not about adding more classes to a schedule — it is about choosing a different direction entirely.

  • Stage presence
  • Confidence under pressure
  • Clean technique
  • Choreography awareness
  • Timing and musicality
  • Ensemble responsibility
  • Performance discipline

Training Focus

What Students
Train in Echo.

01

Aerial Arts

Students develop strength, control, lines, sequencing, and confidence in the air. Training focuses on clean shapes, safe progressions, and performance-ready aerial movement that translates from equipment to stage. Every skill is trained with intention and presentation in mind.

02

Acrobatics

Students build dynamic movement quality through partner-inspired shapes, floor acrobatics, transitions, balance, and controlled physical expression. Acrobatics in Echo is always connected to performance intent — not just what the body can do, but how it does it.

03

Tumbling

Students refine tumbling skills with an emphasis on clean execution, power, control, and the ability to connect skills into performance choreography. Every pass is trained with stage awareness in mind — how you land is as important as what you throw.

The Standard

Training With
a Purpose.

Echo gives students a reason to train beyond the next skill. Every class connects technique to performance quality. Students learn that how they enter, present, transition, recover, and finish matters just as much as the skill itself.

Performance discipline is built over time through repetition, coaching, and real stage experience. Echo students don't just collect skills — they learn to use them in front of an audience, under pressure, with intention. That is a fundamentally different kind of training.

"Echo is where Elemental Athletes learn to perform."

Performance Opportunities

Real Opportunities
to Perform.

Echo students have opportunities to perform throughout the year at Elemental 5 showcases, community events, and larger performance experiences. Elemental 5 has attended VIVA Fest for the last two years, giving students a window into a broader world of youth circus, aerial, and acrobatic performance. Echo membership connects students to these experiences — and to the possibility of more.

Please Note

Participation in specific events may depend on readiness, commitment, and coach approval. Not all Echo students will perform in every event.

Year-Round

Elemental 5 Showcases

Echo students perform in multiple showcase events throughout the year — in front of a real audience, with real choreography and a real stage. This is where training becomes performance. These are the events Echo trains for.

Community

Echo students represent Elemental 5 at local and community events, bringing polished aerial and acrobatic performance beyond the gym walls. These opportunities build confidence and reinforce what it means to perform for a live audience.

Fit & Readiness

Who Echo
Is For.

Echo is for students who are excited by performance and ready for a higher level of commitment. Students do not need to be perfect performers before joining — but they should be coachable, consistent, focused, and willing to train as part of a team.

  • Students who love aerial, acrobatics, or tumbling
  • Students interested in performing in front of an audience
  • Students ready for more focused training
  • Students who can listen, repeat, refine, and work as part of a group
  • Students who want to represent Elemental 5 through performance
