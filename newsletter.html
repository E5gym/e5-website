NinFit Progression Update

Updated Student Skill Log Booklets

Many students already have the first iteration of our NinFit Student Skill Log Booklet. We are releasing an updated version that better matches our current weekly training cycle, progression system, and testing pathway for Levels 1–3.

The booklet is not required. All students continue training, learning, and progressing with or without one. The booklet is simply a high-quality tool for students who enjoy tracking goals, collecting coach sign-offs, and seeing the path from beginner NinFit toward intermediate training.

Booklets are $15 and will be available at the front desk. If your student already has skills signed off in the first version, those skills can be transferred into the updated booklet. Sign-offs happen naturally over time when coaches see that a skill is consistent, controlled, and ready.
