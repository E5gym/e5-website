The Framework

Not just a gym.
A system.

Every skill, every class, every progression at E5 is organized around five elemental principles. Physical and personal development woven together from day one.

Earth
Strength · Stability · Showing up
Foundation-building. Consistency over flash. Strength comes from showing up physically and mentally even when it's hard.
"Show up. Do the work."
Water
Adaptability · Flow · Resilience
When a skill doesn't come easily, water-thinking means adjusting your approach rather than forcing or quitting.
"Bend. Don't break."
Fire
Intensity · Courage · Full effort
Fire moments are when kids commit fully to a scary skill, a hard conversation, or something they've never tried before.
"Go all in."
Air
Creativity · Lightness · Expression
Once foundations are solid, air is the freedom to explore, invent, and bring a unique style to movement.
"Make it yours."
Void
Presence · Awareness · Potential
Stillness within movement. The ability to focus, reset, and be fully in the moment with yourself and others.
"Be here. Be aware."

What We Offer

Four paths.
One framework.

Every program runs on the Five Elements system. The physical skills are the medium. Character development is the point. Not every program is for every kid — and that's by design.

Ages 5–12  ·  Most Popular
NinFit
Parkour · Tumbling · Ninja

The flagship E5 program. NinFit fuses parkour, tumbling, trampoline, and ninja training into a 9-level progression. Kids earn levels through real skill, not time served. Recreational through competitive.

  • Parkour vaults, precision jumps, and flow
  • Tumbling and acrobatic foundations
  • Trampoline and aerial awareness
  • 9-level structured progression
  • Feeds toward Surge for competitive athletes
Ages 5–12
Tx3
Trampoline · Tumbling · Tricking

For kids who can't get enough flips. Tx3 is its own complete curriculum — the full hour spent on trampoline mechanics, ground tumbling, and tricking. Distinct identity from NinFit with its own progression system.

  • Trampoline mechanics and air awareness
  • Tumbling from foundations to advanced
  • Tricking: kicks, flips, and combos
  • Own 5-level progression system
  • Cross-over with NinFit is natural and welcome
Ages 8+
Aerial Silks
Grace · Strength · Expression

A completely distinct track drawing a different kind of athlete. Aerial silks builds extraordinary upper-body strength, spatial awareness, and artistic expression. Beginner classes are open. Intermediate is by invitation.

  • Climbs, wraps, inversions, and drops
  • Choreography and performance readiness
  • Strength conditioning built into every class
  • Beginner open · Intermediate invite-only
  • Feeds naturally toward E5 Echo
Ages 6+
EMA
Elemental Martial Arts

The martial path. EMA blends BJJ, Judo, Karate, Kickboxing, and Muay Thai under one functional philosophy. Real self-defense capability alongside the discipline and respect of classical martial training.

  • Five source arts, one integrated system
  • Functional self-defense from day one
  • Disciplined, traditional structure
  • Its own complete progression pathway
  • Cross-over with movement programs natural
Air · Performance
E5 Echo

Elite performance troupe. Year-round commitment with live shows and travel. Draws expressive athletes across programs. Audition and panel interview required — not a program you enroll in.

Water · Competition
Surge

Competition development track for NinFit and Tx3 athletes headed toward local, state, and national parkour and ninja events. Invitation based on demonstrated commitment and skill.

Leadership
Junior Coach

An 8-week service-based cycle for athletes ages 11–13. Students assist coaches, support younger athletes, and earn their first leadership credential. Open to athletes from any program.

Progression

The journey
doesn't end.

Every program has a real progression system. Your kid isn't just attending class — they're climbing something. Tap a program below to see what that looks like.

Levels 1 – 3
Beginner

Foundational movement literacy. Safe landings, basic vaults, introductory tumbling, and trampoline awareness. Building the Earth layer — showing up and doing the work.

Levels 4 – 6
Intermediate

Real skill development. More complex vaults, back handsprings, aerial awareness drills, and the beginning of personal style. Fire and Air elements start to show up in training.

Levels 7 – 9
Advanced

High performance movement. Competition-ready skills, creative combination runs, and leadership within the gym. Athletes at this level are often on the radar for Surge and Echo.

Beyond Level 9

Advanced NinFit athletes are candidates for Surge (competition), Echo (performance), Junior Coach (leadership), or all three — depending on where their drive takes them.
