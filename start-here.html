First class questions

What parents
want to know.

What if my child is nervous?

That is normal. Coaches help students enter the class at their own pace. The first goal is participation, safety, and comfort.

Does my child need experience?

No. Most students begin with little or no formal experience. Our beginner programs are built around progression.

What should they wear?

Comfortable athletic clothing. Students should bring water and be ready to move. Avoid jeans, jewelry, or clothing that restricts movement.

What if we choose the wrong class?

We can adjust. If a student clearly belongs in a different program or level, our coaches will help guide the next step.

Ready to
begin?

The parent portal is the fastest way to enroll. If you are unsure, send us a message and we’ll help you find the right fit.
