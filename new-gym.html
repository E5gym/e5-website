Why We're Building

This is for
Chaffee County.

Help us build the next generation of youth development.

E5 is not building a franchise. We are building something deep in this community. The long-term vision is simple: every local child eventually benefits from what we do here.

More programs. More kids.

The new facility allows us to expand every program and add new ones, including Elemental Martial Arts and advanced training tracks.

Right here in our backyard.

The building process takes place directly behind the current E5 gym space. Same location. Same team. Bigger mission.

Built to last for years.

Why it matters

E5 was built on a simple belief: movement training is one of the most powerful environments we have for developing young people — not just physically, but in character, discipline, and the ability to face something hard and stay in it.

"Stronger bodies. Sharper minds. Better people."

No facility like this exists within driving distance of Salida. When it opens, it becomes a regional asset — the kind of infrastructure families consider when deciding where to plant roots.
