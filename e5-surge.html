What Surge Is

A high-performance pipeline for competition-ready athletes

Surge is the advanced track within Elemental 5 for parkour athletes who are ready to move beyond general training. It teaches purposeful movement, polished runs, and mental resilience so athletes can compete confidently in local contests and regional Colorado events.

Structured progression

Each cycle is built around measurable gains — from technical precision and vault efficiency to performance pacing and recovery. Surge athletes follow a clear, coach-led path rather than improvising on training day.

Competition-first focus

Every session includes drills that replicate competitive demands: tight transitions, dynamic linking, judges’ expectations, and run-through pressure. The goal is not only to train harder, but to perform smarter.

Who It's For

Strong recovery habits

What Training Looks Like

Precision, power, and performance preparation

Surge training is organized around the three pillars of competitive development: technical mastery, run refinement, and performance mindset. Athletes train with purpose every session so that skill, strength, and strategy come together on the day of competition.

Technical intensity

Focus on exact vault mechanics, landing control, grip transitions, and clean movement language. This phase reduces wasted motion and improves how athletes connect obstacles under speed.

Run integration

Practice full competitive run sequences, link obstacles with intent, and build endurance for multiple rounds. Coaches design run work to mirror local competition formats and scoring expectations.

Strength & resilience

Conditioning supports stronger takeoffs, safer landings, and dependable stamina. Surge blends bodyweight power, jump prep, and recovery mobility for athletes who train hard across the week.

Competition preparation

Includes mock judging, mental rehearsal, and strategy talks so athletes feel calm and confident under stress. Surge makes competition day feel familiar rather than overwhelming.
