Elemental 5 is a youth movement and human development center in Salida, Colorado. Elite acrobatic training and real character development built together, by design.

An Elemental Athlete

More than a skilled mover.

  • Stands on something real. Strength and discipline that holds under pressure.
  • Moves with others and adapts. Flow, resilience, and the ability to adjust.
  • Acts with intensity and follows through. Full commitment when it counts.
  • Stays open, aware, and evolving. Creativity and presence over ego.
  • Integrates it all into who they are. Not just an athlete. A developed person.

The Problem

Most youth programs stop at activity.

Kids stay busy. Parents feel good. Everyone moves on. There is nothing wrong with that, but it leaves something significant on the table. Physical training, done right, is one of the most powerful tools for developing a young person. It teaches resilience. It builds identity. It shows a child what they are actually capable of when they stop backing down from hard things. Most gyms never tap into that.

What We Do Differently

We built E5 specifically to.

We pull from gymnastics, parkour, tumbling, trampoline, and martial arts not to be a version of any of them, but to build something more complete. Every skill, every progression, every class is guided by the Five Element framework. Your child does not just progress through skills. They progress through a system, and they know exactly where they stand, what they have earned, and what comes next.

The Framework

Five elements.
One system.

Each element develops the athlete physically and personally from foundational strength through to mastery and the ability to lead others.

Earth

Strength. Stability. Showing up.

Water

Adaptability. Flow. Resilience.

Fire

Intensity. Courage. Full effort.

Air

Creativity. Lightness. Expression.

Void

Presence. Awareness. Potential.
