Annual Membership Fee

One fee.
All year.

What it covers

Industry-standard annual fee

Annual membership fees help cover unexpected facility and equipment costs, seasonal attendance fluctuations, insurance, staff background checks, student records, and our iClassPro system. It is a necessary component of E5's stability as we continue to grow.

Single student

$30

per year

Multi-student family

$50

per year

Fee waived for active and retired military, firefighters, police, first responders, and teachers. Email us to confirm your eligibility before enrollment.

How it works

Active families are any account with one or more students currently enrolled in a program, class, or camp. Holding only a punch pass does not qualify.
Multi-student rate applies when multiple student profiles exist under a single family account, even if only one is enrolled.
12-month cycle: once charged, you will not be billed again for 12 months. Temporary un-enrollment and re-enrollment within that window does not restart the cycle.
Camp enrollment triggers the fee. If a camp is booked in February for June, the fee is billed in June when the camp runs.
Mid-month enrollment: if you join after billing runs, the membership fee is charged on the following billing period.
